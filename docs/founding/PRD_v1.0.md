\# Mission



\*\*Stop Applying.\*\*



\*\*Start Building Connections.\*\*



Northbound is not a job platform.



It's a platform for becoming.



Our mission is to help builders become the kind of people great teams are excited to build with.



We do this by helping people discover remarkable companies, build authentic professional relationships, continuously improve themselves, and create opportunities through genuine human connection—not transactional networking.



\---



\# Problem



Today's career tools optimize for applications.



More resumes.



More ATS keywords.



More autofill.



More dashboards.



More rejection.



They measure activity.



Not growth.



Networking platforms have the opposite problem.



They give people millions of profiles.



But almost no guidance.



Most people don't know:



\- Who should I reach out to?

\- Why this person?

\- What should I say?

\- How do I start a genuine conversation?

\- How do I stay in touch?

\- How do I build trust?

\- How do I provide value?



As a result...



People either spam recruiters...



or never reach out at all.



Neither builds meaningful relationships.



\---



\# Vision



Northbound becomes the relationship operating system for builders.



Not another job board.



Not another AI application bot.



A place where builders discover companies they genuinely admire...



Meet people they genuinely respect...



Learn from remarkable people...



Build meaningful relationships...



And become the kind of people others genuinely want on their team.

\## 5. Personalized Outreach



Generate thoughtful outreach drafts.



Never generic.



Never spam.



Never automatically sent.



Every draft should feel like it was written by a curious human being.



Northbound drafts the message.



The user always owns the final words.

\- Follow-ups

\- Advice received

\- Last interaction

\# Product Principles



Every feature should answer \*\*YES\*\* to at least one.



\- Helps users build authentic relationships.

\- Helps users become better builders.

\- Encourages curiosity.

\- Encourages learning.

\- Saves meaningful time.

\- Removes friction without removing humanity.

\- Encourages consistency.

\- Helps users create value for others.



If not...



Don't build it.
