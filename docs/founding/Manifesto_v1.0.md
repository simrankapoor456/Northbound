\# The Northbound Manifesto

\## Stop Applying.

\## Start Building Connections.

\---

Somewhere along the way...

we started believing careers are built by sending more applications.

So we applied.

Again.

And again.

And again.

Hundreds of resumes.

Dozens of cover letters.

Endless job boards.

Refresh.

Refresh.

Refresh.

For many people, the job search stopped feeling hopeful.

It became exhausting.

It became transactional.

It became lonely.

Maybe your 2,435th application lands you the job.

Maybe it's your fifth.

Maybe it's the conversation you had six months earlier.

Maybe it's an open-source contribution.

Maybe it's someone remembering your kindness.

Maybe it's a project you almost gave up on.

You'll probably never know.

\*\*We don't optimize for applications.\*\*

\*\*We optimize for becoming.\*\*

\---

Most career platforms sell certainty.

"Get more interviews."

"Land your dream job."

"Increase your response rate."

Those are attractive promises.

But they're also outcomes nobody can fully control.

Northbound believes something different.

We won't promise you a job.

Nobody honestly can.

What we \*can\* promise is this:

We'll help you become a better builder.

A better teammate.

A better communicator.

A better learner.

A better version of yourself.

Whether that leads to your next job...

Your next mentor...

Your next co-founder...

A lifelong friend...

Or an opportunity you never expected...

We can't know.

But we believe becoming is never wasted.

Growth compounds.

Trust compounds.

Curiosity compounds.

Relationships compound.

And so do opportunities.

\*\*The person you become stays with you for the rest of your life.\*\*

\---

We believe careers are built through people.

Through curiosity.

Through consistency.

Through generosity.

Through remarkable work.

Because people don't hire resumes.

People hire people.

And people remember how you made them feel.

\---

Relationships aren't something you collect.

They're not numbers.

They're not followers.

They're not LinkedIn connections.

They're not future referrals.

They're people.

People with dreams.

Stories.

Families.

Challenges.

Ideas.

People who have walked a different path than you.

People you can learn from.

People you can grow alongside.

People you might one day help.

Or who might one day help you.

Not because either of you expected it.

But because that's what genuine relationships naturally become.

\---

Relationships aren't something you build to get ahead.

They're something you build because people matter.

Because you're curious.

Because you're becoming a builder.

Because you value people.

Because you believe every person has something to teach you.

And every conversation is an opportunity to understand the world a little differently.

You celebrate their wins.

You learn from their experiences.

You stay curious about who they are.

You show up.

You listen.

You help when you can.

Not because you're keeping score.

Not because you expect something in return.

But because that's the kind of person you aspire to become.

\*\*People are not opportunities.\*\*

\*\*They're people.\*\*

If an opportunity grows from a genuine relationship...

That's wonderful.

But it was never the purpose.

It was simply the byproduct of two people building something real together.

\---

We don't believe networking should feel transactional.

It shouldn't begin with:

> "Can you refer me?"

It should begin with:

> "I'd love to learn from your experience."

The best conversations begin with curiosity.

Not convenience.

Not desperation.

\---

We don't believe success is measured by the number of applications you send.

Or the number of interviews you get.

Or the company name on your resume.

We believe success is measured by the person you're becoming.

The teammate people trust.

The engineer people remember.

The builder people rely on.

The kind of person others are genuinely excited to build with.

\---

We believe remarkable builders create remarkable opportunities.

Not because they're lucky.

But because they consistently choose to learn.

To build.

To share.

To help.

To stay curious.

To invest in people.

Every relationship.

Every project.

Every conversation.

Every lesson.

Every failure.

Every success.

Becomes another brick.

\---

Northbound exists to help people become the kind of builders they aspire to be.

Because careers are built by people.

And the person always comes before the career.

\*\*Opportunities often grow from the relationships you build...\*\*

\*\*and who you become along the way.\*\*

\---

Technology should never replace human relationships.

It should make them easier to create.

Northbound isn't here to automate connection.

It's here to remove friction.

Not humanity.

\---

Your career is more than your resume.

It's the problems you've solved.

The projects you've built.

The lessons you've learned.

The people you've helped.

The trust you've earned.

The impact you've left behind.

\*\*That's the story worth telling.\*\*

\---

\*\*We believe every builder has their own North Star.\*\*

For some...

It's becoming an ML engineer.

For others...

It's building a startup.

Publishing research.

Leading a team.

Teaching.

Contributing to open source.

Changing someone's life through software.

Northbound doesn't define success.

You do.

Our purpose is simply to walk alongside you as you move toward your North Star.

\---

We're building more than software.

We're building a community.

A place where builders help builders.

Where curiosity is celebrated.

Where questions are encouraged.

Where knowledge is shared.

Where success isn't a competition.

It's something we help each other achieve.

\---

We hope Northbound becomes the place where someone meets their mentor.

Finds their co-founder.

Discovers their dream team.

Builds lifelong friendships.

Creates something remarkable.

Or simply remembers why they started building in the first place.

If we can help create those moments...

We've succeeded.

\---

We're not trying to build the mountain.

We're trying to become the kind of builders who can build one.

One brick at a time.

Together.

\---

\*\*Stop Applying.\*\*

\*\*Start Building Connections.\*\*

Build remarkable things.

Build meaningful relationships.

Help others.

Stay curious.

Keep learning.

Become the engineer great teams are excited to build with.

\*\*Keep becoming.\*\*

\---

\*\*Still moving North.\*\*
